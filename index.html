About Me.

I'm Yaseen ibn Akhtar, a multi-domain Computer Engineer with expertise in cybersecurity, AI, embedded systems, web development, and technical leadership. I design and develop secure systems, intelligent electronics, scalable web platforms, and AI-powered solutions, all with precision and strategic intent. My tech stack includes C++, Python, HTML/CSS/JS, Flask, MATLAB, Linux, Proteus, Arduino, SQL/MySQL, and tools like Suricata, JoeSandbox, and the ELK Stack.

I lead with vision, as the Founder of Logic Vortex and a front-line contributor to real-world problem-solving in defense tech, automation, and cybersecurity. Every system I build is a blend of logic, resilience, and strategic foresight.
